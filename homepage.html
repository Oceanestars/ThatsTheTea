What's The Tea?

Equality. Compassion. Sympathy. Self-Love. Self-Expression. Justice. Peace. Happiness.

That's The Tea.

That's The Tea is a platform built to encourage college students to learn and talk about important social issues. The site includes the latest news on issues such as The Black Lives Matter Campaign, Suicide Prevention, GBTQ+ Inclusion, and Anti-Racism and Equality efforts today. Along with providing resources to increase awareness, get involved, or get help, That's The Tea provides a safe area for individuals to discuss and ask questions about what is going on in the world through a monitored forum space for each individual social issue.

Wanna get involved? Visit our organizations page to see a list of social impact organizations at the university you attend. See how you can get involved now!
