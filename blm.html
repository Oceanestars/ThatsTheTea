Black Lives Matter Movement

Black Lives Matter (BLM) was a phrase coined on Twitter after the acquittal of charges of George Zimmerman after he unjustly shot Trayvon Martin in Ferguson, Missouri in 2013. After the murder of George Floyd and Breonna Taylor, BLM gained huge traction in 2020, sparking international protests to hold police forces accountable. This social movement empowers Black voices to speak on behalf of racially motivated forms of violence, particularly by governments and police forms. BLM strives to correct all forms of injustice engraved within our racist society, from prison reform to education reform, to create ways in which we empower Black voices and disestablish structures of white supremacy.
