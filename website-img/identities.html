LGBTQ+ Awareness

The LGBTQIA+ community represents all gender and sexual minorities. While earlier movements have appeared for generations, many credit the Stonewall Riots and BIPOC voices for sparking the modern fight for equality. Modern movements look to end all forms of oppression from workplace discrimination, to marriage equality, to representation on all forms of media. The movement seeks to passionately engage the public eye to understand the complexities of gender and sexual orientation.
