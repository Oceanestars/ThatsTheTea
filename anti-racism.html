Anti-racism Movement

Antiracism is the active engagement of breaking down racism and oppressive structures engraved within society. These conscious efforts rise out of the need to correct injustices formulated by white supremacy. To be actively antiracist means fighting for all forms of inequalities, but particularly formulating ways in which one may understand how racism continually benefits or hinders their everyday experiences.
