About us

Dare to be AWARE. Dare to SHARE. Dare to CARE.

That's the tea focuses on sharing resoures to improve awareness as well as building community centered around integrity and diversity. Our team is founded on the willingness to help others. Our goal is to provide a central place for people to come together and to have a respectful conversation surrounding important issues. It's sometimes hard to find groups or other people who are willing to make a change. Social Media has divided so many people and we want That's The Tea to bring people together for one thing and one thing only and that is to make the world a better place. With all the issues constantly happening it is easy to feel helpless and not knowing what to do. We hope to alleviate some of that uncertainty.
